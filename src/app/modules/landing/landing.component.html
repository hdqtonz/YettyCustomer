About yetty
Yetty is a digital menu and serving optimization cloud-based web service for restaurants, diners, bars and other kinds of food establishments. It aims to make ordering food and drinks in any establishment easy for customers and personnel and to reduce significantly the trips waiters and other personnel have to make in order to serve customers.
